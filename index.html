Personal Profile

Award-Winning Geospatial Solutions Architect

I am an award-winning geospatial solutions architect with more than 8 years of experience in academia and industry. I am passionate about building data-driven applications using free and open-source software, open standards, and open data. My professional experience includes designing, building, and maintaining geospatial software solutions that convert complex data into information and insight for different industries from urban planning to the energy industry. Over the course of my career, I have provided technical leadership to cross-functional development teams, implemented software development best practices, and collaborated with developers, analysts, and executives to build innovative software products.
